To get your data:

  1. Open the Tik Tok app
  2. Tap the "profile" icon at the bottom.
  3. Press the 3 bars on the upper right corner.
  4. Press Settings and Privacy.
  5. Tap Account.
  6. Go to "Download your data".
  7. Change the file format by tapping on "Select file format".
  8. Switch to JavaScript Object Notation (JSON).
  9. After waiting a while, you should receive a system notification that looks like this:
  10. Tap the update, then view more. That will take you to this page.
  11. You will be redirected to a browser. Continue with the download by tapping "Download".
  12. Tap the blue arrow in the bottom left and save to device files.

Tada! You should be ready to upload your data to the original page.
